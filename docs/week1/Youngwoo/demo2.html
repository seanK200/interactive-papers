Mavhu F.W. Hargrove, “Guest Voices: Shona Creation Story,” on “Cosmos Diary,”africancosmosdiary.wordpress.com/ 2012/05/18/guest-voicesshona-creation-story/ (May 18, 2012)


God created Mwedzi (the moon) in a deep pool of water. The moon begged to be allowed to live on land. Once on land, Mwedzi was lonely, so God sent Hweva (Morningstar) down to become his wife but warned that after two years he would have to return her to the sky. Hweva stayed with Mwedzi and gave birth to all the vegetation on earth. After two years, a reluctant Mwedzi sent her back to the sky. Mwedzi became lonely again, so God sent him Venekatsvimborume (Eveningstar) and again told him that she must return to the sky after two years. Venekatsvimborume stayed with Moon. She gave birth, first to herbivores and birds and then to boys and girls. After two years God asked the Mwedzi to return her. Mwedzi refused. On the next day Venekatsvimborume gave birth to lions, scorpions and other predators. [Click here for a video of this reading]

Mwari vakasika Mwedzi (mwedzi) mudziva rakadzika remvura. Mwedzi wakakumbirisa kuti ubvumidzwe kugara pasi. Pane imwe nguva pamunda, Mwedzi akasurukirwa, saka Mwari vakatumira Hweva (Morningstar) pasi kuti azove mukadzi wake asi akanyevera kuti mushure memakore maviri aifanira kumudzosera kudenga. Hweva akagara naMwedzi ndokubereka kubereka kwese kusango pasi. Mushure memakore maviri, anomira Mwedzi akamudzosera kudenga. Mwedzi akasurukirwa zvekare, saka Mwari vakamutumira Venekatsvimborume (Eveningstar) uye vakamuudza zvakare kuti anofanira kudzokera kudenga mushure memakore maviri. Venekatsvimborume yakagara neMwedzi. Iye akazvara, kutanga kune herbivores uye shiri uye kune vakomana nevasikana. Mushure memakore maviri Mwari akakumbira VaMwedzi kuti vamudzore. Mwedzi akaramba. Zuva rakatevera racho Vekekatsvimborume vakabereka shumba, zveshuwa nezvimwe zvikara. [Click here for a video of this reading]
