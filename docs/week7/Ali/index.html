Court Transcript: SCT 5-4-19 Regina V. Quamina Eddoo, 10 Nov 1876

Acting Judicial Assessor William Melton Presiding

Abinah Mansah, having promised and declared that she would speak the truth says:

ABINA MANSAH A man called Yowawhah brought me from Ashantee. I was his wife. He brought me to Salt Pond. Yowawhah went on purchasing goods. On the same day as he finished, he handed me over to defendant to be with him, and said that he was going back and would return.
