BRABANTIO 60 Ay, to me; 60 She is abus'd, stol'n from me, and corrupted 61 By spells and medicines bought of mountebanks; 62 For nature so preposterously to err, 63 Being not deficient, blind, or lame of sense, 64 Sans witchcraft could not.
